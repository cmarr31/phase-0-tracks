How to use an elevator:
- Enter elevator.
- Decide what floor you would like the elevator to take you to.
- Press the number corresponding to the number of the floor you would like to go to.
- Wait until the elevator reaches the floor you wanted to go to.
- Exit elevator.

How to brush teeth:
- Put adequate amount of toothpaste on the end of toothbrush.
- Run toothpaste on the end of toothbrush under water from sink.
- Put end of toothbrush with toothpaste on it in mouth.
- Rub your teeth with brush and toothpaste for two minutes or until all teeth have been rubbed 5x.
- Spit out foam/toothpaste.
- Wash mouth out with water.
